**Goal:** Education — make them aware the model exists

```

Most of it went to 3 platforms.

to a model that pays only for results.

[BODY]

→ Content Rewards model: ₹80,000 budget. 150+ creator-posted clips. Pay only per 1,000 views delivered.
→ One brand. Same budget. 47x more content touchpoints.

The math isn't complicated. The model just hasn't reached India yet.

I'm building the platform that brings it here — specifically for Indian brands,
INR payouts, regional language creators.

[CTA]
Would this model make sense for your brand's next product launch?
Drop a comment or DM me — happy to show you how one campaign could look for [your category].
```

**Why this works:** Specific rupee number in hook forces explanation. 47x claim = forces verification-seeking behavior in comments.

## Template 3 — The Mini Case Study Post

**Use when:** After your first pilot campaign (even small numbers work)
**Key:** Always include the Meta CPM comparison — that's what lands

Current Meta benchmark to use: ₹500–800 CPM for D2C/e-commerce on Instagram

```
[HOOK]
We ran a 10-day content rewards pilot for a [city]-based [category] brand.
Here's what happened.

[BODY]
Budget allocated: ₹25,000
Creators who posted: 63
Total clips published: 63 (across Reels, Shorts, and YouTube)
Combined views in 10 days: 4,10,000
Effective CPM paid: ₹61

For context: Instagram Reels ads in India currently average ₹500–800 CPM.

They paid 8x less per 1,000 views — and got authentic creator content instead of an ad.

This is what a performance-based UGC model looks like when it's built for India.

[CTA]
Running 3 more pilots this month — DM me if your brand wants in.
```

**Note on fabricating numbers:** Don't make these up. Even a small real pilot (20 creators, 50K views) beats imaginary numbers. Your first post using this template should be real.
